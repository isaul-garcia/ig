Bonjour! Ana is a subscription based cleaning service.

The company wanted to re-establish their whole branding strategy.

Gabriela Enid and I where hired as designers to do just that. We where only given the logo and its initial color. Then we worked together to conceptualize the identity and tone of the whole company.
