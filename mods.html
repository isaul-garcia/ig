MODs
BA ❘ 2016 ❘ Urban public computing system.
ObjectUrbanismPlace ComputingConceptArchitecture

Cities are important nodes of information and human interaction. MODs was designed as a hardware and software platform that would help the adoption of smart city technologies to public spaces and its inhabitants. The platform itself empowers citizens to understand how this technologies work and how they can be beneficial.

Part of its purpose is to have an explicit operating system that would help people be aware and take better decisions when it comes to public computing.
