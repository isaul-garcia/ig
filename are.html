AR-E
MA ❘ 2019 ❘ Doodling relationships in the natural ecosystem.
Augmented RealityArtConceptualInfographic

With an augmented reality paint app, I began doodling relationships in the natural ecosystem, revealing information of its own unintentional links. AR-E served as a representation of life cycles. This was later replicated on a human environment, evidencing the lack of self continuance. The human is in charge of all responsabilities to maintain this closed environment. The environment remains unresponsive of its own or neighboring necessities.

Its design is simple with round corners. When combined with different materials and patterns the possibilities are endless.
